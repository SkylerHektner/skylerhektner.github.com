About Me

I'm a C++ gameplay programmer at Grinding Gear Games working on the online Action RPG Path of Exile. I studied computer science and game design at the University of Utah under the Eccles Distinguished Merit Scholarship. I also grew up in Alaska, so feel free to ask me about it! (Yes, you should visit. No, not in the winter.).

I'm a gamer and technical enthusiast at heart with a huge passion for content creation and team based work. Six years in competitive debate and countless late nights working on various side projects have made me no stranger to self-teaching. At the end of the day, though, my main goal is simply to create things I'm proud of while surrounded by people I enjoy.
