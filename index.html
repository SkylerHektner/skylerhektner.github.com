About Me

I'm an Associate Gameplay Engineer with Bungie working on the Action MMO Destiny 2. I studied computer science and game design at the University of Utah under the Eccles Distinguished Merit Scholarship. I also grew up in Alaska, so feel free to ask me about it! (Yes, you should visit. No, not in the winter. Unless you love darkness and cold).

I'm a gamer and technical enthusiast at heart with a huge passion for content creation and team based work. Countless side projects and game jams alongside six years in competitive debate has made me no stranger to self-teachings. At the end of the day my main goal in life is simply to create things I'm proud of while surrounded by people I enjoy.
